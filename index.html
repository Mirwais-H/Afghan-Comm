Welcome to Afghan Link

Afghan Link is a vibrant community platform that connects Afghans living in Minnesota. Whether you're looking for the latest news, community events, resources, or simply a place to connect with fellow Afghans, Afghan Link is here for you. Join us as we build a supportive and inclusive community together.
